Introduction

Miracle on 22nd Street, Inc. (referred to throughout this policy as “Miracle”, “we” or “us”) is a nonprofit corporation. Through the website operated at miracleon22ndstreet.com and any other websites under our control (collectively the “Websites”), Miracle seeks to connect families in need with an army of “elves” dedicated to helping children fulfill their holiday wishes. The process may involve obtaining and utilizing personal information from the users of our Websites, in the ways described in this privacy policy (“Privacy Policy”). This Privacy Policy applies to the personal information we collect about you through the Websites, regardless of media platform (e.g. computer, smartphone, tablet, etc.).

Your privacy is important to us, and this Privacy Policy is designed to make clear our intentions and approach to the collection and use of personal information in connection with our Websites and the choices you have. If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at miracleon22ndstreet@gmail.com

You also have the option of reviewing, updating, and deleting Personal Information you have submitted by contacting us at miracleon22ndstreet@gmail.com

PROTECTING CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

We strongly believe in protecting the privacy of children. Our website is not directed toward children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Other than collecting the information necessary from parents and/or legal guardians to enable us to fulfill the wish lists of children in need, we do not knowingly collect or maintain any Personal Information on our Websites about persons under 13 years of age. Upon learning of the existence of Personal Information concerning persons less than 13 years of age that has been collected on our Websites without verified parental consent, we will take appropriate steps to delete it. If you are concerned that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at miracleon22ndstreet@gmail.com to review, modify or delete such information.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT & HOW WE COLLECT IT

For the purposes of operating, improving, and monitoring the user experience on our Websites and our operations generally, we collect various information from people using the Websites. This information is broadly of two types: 1. Personal Information (information which is personal to you and can be used to identify you, such as your name and email address) 2. General Information (information which cannot be used to identify you, such as the number of users—but not their names—who have visited the Websites).

General Information & Cookies. When you access our Websites, we may collect General Information including, but not limited to, pages visited, hyperlinks used, type of device used, date, and time spent on our Websites.

Collecting such General Information may involve logging the IP address, operating system and browser software used by individual site visitors. From such information a user’s Internet Service Provider and other data could be inferred, but cannot be used to personally identify you. We use this information to understand how to service users of our Websites and improve our Websites and programs by using Google Analytics or other website analysis tools.

We may also use cookies and/or other technologies to store and help track information about you to provide you with a customized and personal service. Most browsers are initially set to accept such technologies and offer easy instructions on setting your browser to reject some or all of them. For example, you may choose to delete cookies at any time. However, please note that various personalized functions and benefits of the Websites may not work properly if you reject our cookies or other web technologies.

We do not track visitors across third party websites to provide targeted advertising, and therefore do not respond to Do Not Track (DNT) signals.

Personal Information. You can access content on our Websites without revealing any Personal Information. We only collect Personal Information when you provide it to us directly, for example:

  • If you register to join Santa’s List as a family in need of help;
  • If via the Put Me On Santa’s List page, you agree to share your story and any Personal Information on the Facebook Group for Miracle on 22nd Street;
  • If you register to become an Elf in order to fulfill wish lists and letters from families in need.
  • If you register with us to receive email newsletters or other communications from us;
  • If you contact us through the Websites;
  • If you donate to us;
  • If you participate with or provide User Content (as defined in the Terms and Conditions of Use) such as comments on social media accounts operated by Miracle;
  • When we need to verify your account information.
  • When you interact with any Miracle page or account on a social media platform, such as Facebook, Twitter, Google+, LinkedIn, Tumblr, YouTube, or Pinterest (collectively, “Miracle Social Media”), or when you use your social media account or credentials to log into the Websites, we may also collect the Personal Information that you make available to us on that page or through that account.

Collection of Personal Information by Third Party Service Providers. When you make an online donation to Miracle, you will be directed to the Causevox website, and you will be asked to provide Personal Information to Causevox to complete the transaction. At this point, you will be governed by Causevox’ privacy policy.

We use Personal Information in any of the following ways:

To communicate with you, if you have elected to join our mailing list;

  • To investigate any activities that may violate our policies or be illegal;
  • To manage and operate our organization in general.

DISCLOSURE OF PERSONAL INFORMATION

We will not disclose any Personal Information we gather from you through the Websites, except as forth in this Privacy Policy (or specifically agreed to by you).

Elves. If you request a holiday gift through the Websites, we will share your Personal Information with an elf assigned to you. Your assigned elf may use this information to send your family a holiday gift or gifts. Your elf may also communicate with you about your holiday wish through the address or email you have provided. Elves may share this information with third parties in connection with fulfilling a wish by purchasing and sending gifts from an online or other merchant. Under our Terms and Conditions of Use, we require elves to commit to using your Personal Information only for the purposes of sending a holiday gift to requesting families and communication about such gift unless otherwise permitted by you; however, we are not able to and do not take responsibility for controlling the elves’ use of Personal Information.
